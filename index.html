Discover the Legacy of Athamkudi

Nestled in the heart of Chettinad, Athamkudi is a village steeped in history, culture, and timeless beauty. Famous for its traditional Chettinad architecture, this village is home to some of the most exquisite mansions ever built by the Chettiar community renowned merchants and patrons of art and architecture. At the center of Athamkudi's charm lies the Athamkudi Palace, a stunning heritage home that stands as a testament to the craftsmanship of a bygone era. From intricately carved wooden doors to colorful handmade Athangudi tiles, every element of the palace tells a story of elegance, pride, and tradition.

Experience the grandeur of Athamkudi Palace in two unique ways. The Walkthrough is a web-based journey, similar to Google Street View, enriched with visuals and audio that reveal the palace’s rich heritage. The VR Experience offers a fully immersive tour through a headset, bringing the sights and sounds of the palace to life in 360°.
